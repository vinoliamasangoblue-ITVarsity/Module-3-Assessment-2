Tuna Empanadillas

Prep time : 30 minutes

Yield : 4

Ingredients













Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C/180°C fan forced. Line a large baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Heat the oilin a frying pan over medium heat. Add onion and red capsicum and green capsicum. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 3-4 minutes or until softened. Add tomato. Cook, stirring, for 3 minutes or until softened. Stir in paprika. Transfer to a large bowl. Stir in the tuna, olive and capers. Set aside for 1 hour to cool completely.
  3. Use an 8cm round pastry cutter to cut 9 discs from each pastry sheet. Divide the filling among 1 half of each pastry disc. Fold over remaining half to cover filling. Use your fingertips to press edges together to seal, then gently twist to crimp. Transfer to the prepared tray. Brush with a little egg..
  4. Bake for 20 minutes or until golden. Serve warm or at room temperature with the aioli.
